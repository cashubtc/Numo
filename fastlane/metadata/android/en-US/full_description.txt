Numo is an Android Point-of-Sale application that enables merchants to receive Cashu ecash payments via tap-to-pay.

The application acts as a simple point-of-sale terminal for receiving Bitcoin payments. When a customer approaches their phone with a supporting wallet, the app receives the ecash payment over NFC.

How It Works:
The merchant enters the desired amount in satoshis using the application's keypad. When they press "Charge", a payment screen appears and a type-4 forum tag is emulated with a Cashu Payment Request string. The payer's Cashu wallet reads the contents via NFC and writes a Cashu token with the requested amount back to the terminal, and Numo will process it. 

Numo also supports direct Lightning payments. The merchant can switch to the "lightning" tab in the payment screen, generating a fresh BOLT11 invoice displayed as a QR code and transmitted over NFC.

Features:
- Tap-to-pay with Cashu ecash and NDEF protocol
- Lightning BOLT11 invoices support
- Merchant item catalogs with pre-selectable items
- Merchant item baskets that can be persisted
- Payment history and integration for Cashu wallets
- Withdraw to self-custody or Lightning address
- Automatic withdrawal after threshold is reached

Important: This application is a Point-of-Sale terminal. It is intended to receive payments and immediately generate redemption tokens.
